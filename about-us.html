Who We Are

LOMAPAM INVESTMENTS (PTY) LTD Corporation has been incorporated in Swaziland under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2009, as amended (Companies Act). The company has been operating for than ten (10) years. Its started as a company which sells imported cars then as the company grew it bought trucks. As attached below are the current fleet of assets. The business runs its won workshop where all repairs are done to minimuse cost and have a mechanic on stand by. Other car dealers depend on hire of transportation. We also have a workshop which fully operational and have plant & machinery .

Our Mission

To create sustainable value by focusing on a core group of market leading transportation and transportation related businesses, leveraging on innovative capitalisation structures and outcomes-driven programme management, operating primarily through Swaziland Transporters Association. By harnessing innovative global and local funding structures, strong partnerships with Individuals, Companies, Organization, Government and proven systems and communication technology, real delivery in transportation development can be realized.

Our Value System

LOMAPAM INVESTMENTS (PTY) LTD Transport subscribes to the principal of adding significant value in whatsoever sector it is involved. This concept addresses the normal commercial elements of providing competitive advantage over the long term and satisfying clients with exceptional levels of service and quality. LOMAPAM INVESTMENTS (PTY) LTD transport however goes an entire level further by ensuring that the transport solutions it delivers, adds value to the entire spectrum of stake holders involved, in particular by creating measurable impacts within communities.

The quality of service in the transport sector has simply not matched minimum requirements and new standards will have to be set, together with service levels measurement criteria and protocols. LOMAPAM INVESTMENTS (PTY) LTD transport will actively contribute towards the development of new service standards and the maintenance thereof. Our competitors are big companies like Unitrans and Cresildas Transport whose fleet is well established in the transport industry but our company has tapped into the market and have established solids contracts with companies that we are offering our services. Our drivers are highly qualified and well paid. There is no risk of drivers changing jobs.

The outcomes of a transport system cannot be measured as the simple aggregation of its inputs and short term deliverables. LOMAPAM INVESTMENTS (PTY) LTD transport approaches the design and development of any transport solution within the context of assessing the efficiencies and impacts of a particular system, over its entire life cycle.

Generally, empowerment initiatives tend to benefit only a handful of highly visible black organisations or individuals. With this in mind, LOMAPAM INVESTMENTS (PTY) LTD Transport and its associated companies, has chosen a direction that is multi-exclusive and self-sustaining.
